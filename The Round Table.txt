Experience (XP) Scaling Examples
Linear: Each level takes the same amount of XP (e.g., 1000 XP per level).
Exponential: The XP needed increases, requiring more effort for higher levels (e.g., level 2 needs 100, level 3 needs 200, level 4 needs 400).
Cap: A maximum skill level or character level (e.g., Level 120 cap in RuneScape).
