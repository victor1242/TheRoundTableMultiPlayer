Wild Cards

Each round has a different wild rank. The wild rank is the round number plus two.

Jokers are always wild.

Wild cards can stand in for any rank and suit when forming books and runs.

Try to get rid of your wild cards whenever a player goes out.

Penalties for jokers is 50 points and the current round wild card is 20 points.
