Books and Runs

In Five Crowns, you score by forming books and runs.

Book (Set)

A book is three or more cards of the same rank, regardless of suit.

Run

A run is three or more cards in sequence, all of the same suit.

Wild cards and jokers can substitute to complete a book or a run.
