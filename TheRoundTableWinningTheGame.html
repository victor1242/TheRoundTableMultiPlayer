Winning the Game

The Round Table is played over 11 rounds. After the final round of 11, the player with the lowest total score wins the game. Be careful keeping high-value cards in your hand. There is a substantial penalty (50 points for jesters and 20 points for the current round wild card) if you cannot play them on your final play in the round.

Try to gain some idea as to what the next player to your right might be holding based on what they pick up from the discard pile and what they discard.
