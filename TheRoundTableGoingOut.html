Going Out

You may go out when all but one card in your hand can be laid down in books and runs. After melding, you discard your final card to end the round. Other players then get a final turn in the round.
They may also go out. A Bonus of points equal to the round number is awarded to the players who go out in the round. After the end of the round, the total value of cards remaining in each player's hand is added to their score as penalty points.
