Winning the Game

Five Crowns is usually played over 11 rounds.

After the final round, the player with the lowest total score wins.

Keeping high-value cards out of your hand is the key to winning.

Try to gain some idea as to what the next player to your right might be holding based on what they pick up from the discard pile and what they discard.
