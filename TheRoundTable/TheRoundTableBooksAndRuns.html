The Round Table - Books and Runs

In The Round Table, you score by forming books and runs.

Book (Set)

A book is three or more cards of the same rank, regardless of suit. Bonus points are awarded for completing books and runs with NO wild cards or jesters.

Run

A run is three or more cards in sequence, all of the same suit. Wild cards and jesters can substitute to complete a book or a run.
