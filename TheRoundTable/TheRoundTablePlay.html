How to Play The Round Table

The player to the left of the dealer starts the round. Play moves clockwise.

You will begin each of your turns choosing a card to draw. You can either take the top card from the draw or discard pile. Add the card you draw to your hand. Should the draw pile ever run out of cards, the discard pile is shuffled to form a new draw pile.

The objective of The Round Table is to create books and runs (see the Books and Runs page). You can only play cards from your hand. You must be able to discard after playing your turn. If you can use all but one of them in a book and/or a run you go out. If you cannot use all of your cards, you must keep them in your hand. You will then end your turn by discarding one card from your hand to the discard pile. Play then passes to the player on your left unless you are the last player for this round, meaning that the next player was the one who went out.
