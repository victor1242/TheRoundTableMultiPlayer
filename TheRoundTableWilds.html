The Round Table - Wild Cards

Each round has a different wild rank. The wild rank is the round number plus two. For example, in round 1, the wild rank is 3. In round 2, the wild rank is 4, and so on. In round 11, the wild rank is king. Jesters are always wild.
Wild cards can stand in for any rank and suit when forming books and runs. Bonus points are awarded for completing books and runs with NO wild cards or jesters.
Try to get rid of your wild cards whenever a player goes out. Penalties for jesters is 50 points and the current round wild card is 20 points.
