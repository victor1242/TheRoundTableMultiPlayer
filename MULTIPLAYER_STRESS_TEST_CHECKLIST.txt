Five Crowns Multiplayer Stress Test (4 Devices)

Devices
- Victor (laptop)
- Fred (iPad)
- Phone player
- Fire tablet player

Setup
1. Join all 4 devices to one room.
2. Confirm each device shows all players in the room list.

Test Sequence
1. Play 2 full rounds with no disconnects.
2. Disconnect one non-host mid-turn, reconnect after 15-30 seconds.
3. Disconnect host mid-turn, reconnect after 15-30 seconds.
4. Disconnect 2 players at once, reconnect one first, then the other.
5. Keep one player offline for 2-3 turns (AI takeover), then reconnect.

Verify After Each Scenario
1. No "Room not found" appears for remaining players.
2. Turn owner is correct on every device.
3. Reconnected player gets correct hand/state restore.
4. Status banner matches actual current turn.
5. Opponent list includes everyone; offline/AI markers are accurate.

Pass Criteria
1. No frozen turns.
2. No room destruction while at least one player remains connected.
3. No stale status text after turn changes.
4. Scores and round transitions stay consistent on all devices.
